Menemen

Menemen is a Turkish scrambled egg dish with tomatoes, peppers, and spices. Serve hot with bread.

Börek

Börek is a savory pastry filled with cheese, spinach, or meat, often served as a snack or breakfast item.

Kısır

Kısır is a bulgur salad with tomato paste, parsley, lemon juice, and olive oil. Refreshing and light!

Lahmacun

Lahmacun is a thin Turkish flatbread topped with spiced minced meat, tomatoes, and herbs. Fold and eat!

Baklava

Baklava is a sweet pastry made of layers of filo dough, filled with nuts, and soaked in syrup or honey.
